## Recommended Reading Order

### For Complete Beginners
1. [README.md - What is MCP?](README.md#what-is-mcp) (5 min)
2. [GETTING_STARTED.md](GETTING_STARTED.md) (30 min - hands-on)
3. [examples/python/simple_tool.py](examples/python/simple_tool.py) or [examples/typescript/src/simple-tool.ts](examples/typescript/src/simple-tool.ts) (10 min)
4. [LEARNING_PATHS.md - Complete Beginner](LEARNING_PATHS.md#path-1-complete-beginner) (follow over 1-2 hours)

### For Experienced Developers
1. [README.md - What is MCP?](README.md#what-is-mcp) (5 min)
2. [QUICKREF.md](QUICKREF.md) (10 min)
3. Pick relevant examples from [examples/](examples/) (15 min)
4. [LEARNING_PATHS.md](LEARNING_PATHS.md) - Choose your path (follow over 1-2 weeks)

### For Quick Reference While Coding
→ Keep [QUICKREF.md](QUICKREF.md) open
→ Check [examples/](examples/) for patterns

### For Finding Learning Materials
→ Browse [README.md](README.md)
→ Sections: Courses, Videos, Examples, Community Resources
